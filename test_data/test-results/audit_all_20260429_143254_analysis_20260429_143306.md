After remediation of F001 and F002:
- The absence of REQ-NFR-001 and REQ-NFR-002 executable tests remains a known gap. For a PoC with a developer-workstation SUT and stable data-testid attributes (visible from browser), the practical risk is low. For regulated release, these must be closed.
